📖 Brief Biography

I am currently an postdoc in the Department of Computer Science at Tsinghua University. Before that, I obtained my Ph.D. from Central South University in 2025, advised by Prof. Ju Ren. My research interests include Operating System, Networking System, and Device Intelligence. I am a member of the IEEE.
